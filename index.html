Crow

Introduction

Crows are highly intelligent birds that belong to the genus Corvus. They are known for their problem-solving abilities and adaptability.

Habitat

Crows live in forests, cities, and open areas around the world.

Diet

Crows eat insects, fruits, seeds, and small animals.

Behavior

Crows are social and communicate using different sounds.

Intelligence

Crows can use tools and remember human faces.
