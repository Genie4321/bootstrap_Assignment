Crow

Introduction

Crows are highly intelligent birds that belong to the genus Corvus. They are known for their problem-solving abilities and adaptability.

Habitat

Crows are found all over the world, especially in forests, cities, and open landscapes.

Diet

Crows are omnivores. They eat insects, seeds, fruits, and even small animals.

Behavior

Crows are social birds and often live in groups. They communicate using a variety of sounds.

Intelligence

Crows can use tools, recognize human faces, and remember things for a long time.
